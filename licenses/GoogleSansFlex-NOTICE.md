# Google Sans Flex

The bundled `google_sans_flex_*.ttf` files are official Google Fonts static
instances of Google Sans Flex. The font metadata identifies Google as the
designer, marks the family as open source, and links the font to the SIL Open
Font License 1.1. The license text is included in `GoogleSansFlex-OFL.txt`.

Source: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Google+Sans+Flex
